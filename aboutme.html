Computing Background

Personal computing has always been a hobby of mine. From the BASIC days on the Commodore 64 to building my own desktops in high-school, my early days involved computers.

In college I began building Linux machines and networks and learned a great deal about documentation, open source projects and ipconfig.

Surprised by the versatility and power of a Linux system I began trying to convince friends and neighbors to install it on their computers.

This culminated with 2 years volunteering at Free Geek, teaching students how to use GIMP, a feature-rich GNU photo editor.
